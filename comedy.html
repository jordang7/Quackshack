Top 10 Comedy Movies of 2017

Movie Title Cover picture Overview Movie Rating Director
1. Thor: Ragnarok Imprisoned, the mighty Thor finds himself in a lethal gladiatorial contest against the Hulk, his former ally. Thor must fight for survival and race against time to prevent the all-powerful Hela from destroying his home and the Asgardian civilization. 8.3/10 Taika Waititi
2. Kingsman: The Gold Circle When their headquarters are destroyed and the world is held hostage, the Kingsman's journey leads them to the discovery of an allied spy organization in the US. These two elite secret organizations must band together to defeat a common enemy. 7.2/10 Matthew Vaughn
3. The Babysitter The events of one evening take an unexpected turn for the worst for a young boy trying to spy on his babysitter. 6.4/10 McG
4. American Made The story of Barry Seal, an American pilot who became a drug-runner for the CIA in the 1980s in a clandestine operation that would be exposed as the Iran-Contra Affair. 7.3/10 Doug Liman
5. Tyler Perry's Boo 2! A Madea Halloween Madea, Bam, and Hattie venture to a haunted campground and the group must run for their lives when monsters, goblins, and the boogeyman are unleashed. 3.0/10 Tyler Perry
6. The Meyerowitz Stories (New and Selected) An estranged family gathers together in New York for an event celebrating the artistic work of their father. 7.2/10 Noah Baumbach
7. The Emoji Movie Gene, a multi-expressional emoji, sets out on a journey to become a normal emoji. 2.6/10 Tony Leondis
8. The LEGO Ninjago Movie Shunned by everyone for being the son of an evil warlord, a teenager seeks to defeat him with the help of his fellow ninjas. 6.1/10 Charlie Bean, Paul Fisher, Bob Logan
9. Baywatch Devoted lifeguard Mitch Buchannon butts heads with a brash new recruit, as they uncover a criminal plot that threatens the future of the bay. 5.6/10 Seth Gordon
10. Cars 3 Lightning McQueen sets out to prove to a new generation of racers that he's still the best race car in the world. 7.0/10 Brian Fee
