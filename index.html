Top 10 Overall Movies of 2017

Movie Title Cover picture Overview Movie Rating Director
1. Blade Runner 2049 A young blade runner's discovery of a long-buried secret leads him to track down former blade runner Rick Deckard, who's been missing for thirty years. 8.5/10 Denis Villeneuve
2. The Babysitter The events of one evening take an unexpected turn for the worst for a young boy trying to spy on his babysitter. 6.4/10 McG
3. It A group of bullied kids band together when a shapeshifting demon, taking the appearance of a clown, begins hunting children. 7.8/10 Andy Muschietti
4. Thor: Ragnarok Imprisoned, the mighty Thor finds himself in a lethal gladiatorial contest against the Hulk, his former ally. Thor must fight for survival and race against time to prevent the all-powerful Hela from destroying his home and the Asgardian civilization. 8.3/10 Taika Waititi
5. The Snowman Detective Harry Hole investigates the disappearance of a woman whose pink scarf is found wrapped around an ominous-looking snowman. 5.2/10 Tomas Alfredson
6. Geostorm When the network of satellites designed to control the global climate starts to attack Earth, it's a race against the clock to uncover the real threat before a worldwide Geostorm wipes out everything and everyone. 5.8/10 Dean Devlin
7. Happy Death Day A college student must relive the day of her murder over and over again, in a loop that will end only when she discovers her killer's identity. 6.7/10 Christopher Landon
8. Kingsman: The Gold Circle When their headquarters are destroyed and the world is held hostage, the Kingsman's journey leads them to the discovery of an allied spy organization in the US. These two elite secret organizations must band together to defeat a common enemy. 7.2/10 Matthew Vaughn
9. The Foreigner A humble businessman with a buried past seeks justice when his daughter is killed in an act of terrorism. A cat-and-mouse conflict ensues with a government official, whose past may hold clues to the killers' identities. 7.3/10 Martin Campbell
10. American Made The story of Barry Seal, an American pilot who became a drug-runner for the CIA in the 1980s in a clandestine operation that would be exposed as the Iran-Contra Affair. 7.3/10 Doug Liman
